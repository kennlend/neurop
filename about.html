Our Story

We were tired of the same
broken products.

Coffee that makes you anxious. Energy drinks that crash you in two hours. Nicotine pouches marketed as focus tools. Supplement capsules that take an hour to kick in and cost you attention, not buy it back.

NeuroPouch started as a question: what if you built a focus product that actually respected your intelligence? Clean ingredients you can look up. A delivery method that works with your body's biology. No addiction risk. No jitter spike. No crash.

Why We Built This

The market was full of stimulants pretending to be solutions.

How We Work

Four principles. Non-negotiable.

Full transparency, always

No proprietary blends. No "matrix" or "complex" labels hiding underdosed ingredients. Every ingredient, every form, every function — published. If we can't explain it, it doesn't go in.

Zero addiction risk — ever

No nicotine. No taurine. No compounds with dependency profiles. NeuroPouch should be a tool, not a habit you can't break. This is a hard line we will never cross, regardless of what becomes trendy.

Science, not marketing claims

We cite our sources. Our science page links to the actual studies — not summaries of summaries. We won't claim anything we can't back with published research.

Premium European manufacturing

Formulated and manufactured in compliance with EU food supplement regulations. Ingredient sourcing prioritises quality and traceability over cost minimisation. Every tin reflects that.

The Journey

How we got here.

The Problem

High performance, high cost

The founding team were deep in the biohacker world — tracking sleep, HRV, cognitive output. The tools available for mental performance were either outdated (caffeine), risky (nicotine), or ineffective (most "nootropic" blends). The gap was obvious.

The Research

Six months of formulation work

We didn't start with a product. We started with ingredient research — reviewing published studies, consulting with formulators, and building a stack from first principles. Buccal delivery was identified early as the key differentiator — faster, more consistent, more discreet.

The Prototype

First batches. Real feedback.

Beta testing with a closed group of biohackers, students, and professionals. Feedback shaped the final formulation and the two flavour profiles. The peppermint & lemongrass variant came from a clear preference for crisp, clean profiles over sweet or artificial tastes.

Now

Pre-launch. Waitlist open.

The product is ready. First production run underway. We are launching with full transparency — this website, a public science page, and a waitlist for people who want to be first. No hype campaign. No influencer-first rollout. Just the product and the evidence.

Next

RelaxPouch. Wider distribution.

The evening companion formula is in development. Retail and wholesale partnerships are being explored for gyms, co-working spaces, and premium pharmacies across Europe. The long-term mission is to build the defining performance supplement brand of this decade — clean, smart, and honest.

Our Commitments

Things we'll never put in a NeuroPouch.

This is a public commitment. Hold us to it.

✗

Nicotine

Addictive. Cardiovascular risks. Age-restricted. Not in NeuroPouch — ever.

✗

Taurine

Overused in energy drinks. Unclear long-term profile. No place in a clean nootropic stack.

✗

Proprietary blends

A technique to hide low doses behind impressive ingredient lists. We publish everything.

✗

Artificial sweeteners

We use natural flavour profiles only. No sucralose, no aspartame, no acesulfame-K.

✗

Synthetic stimulants

DMAA, synephrine, and similar compounds are banned in many countries for good reason. We don't use them.

✗

Unverifiable claims

If we say an ingredient does something, we link to the study. No vague "supports performance" copy without evidence.
