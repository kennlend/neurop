One-line description

NeuroPouch is a nicotine-free, EU-manufactured neuro functional oral pouch, formulated for focus and cognitive performance, combining caffeine, L-theanine, Alpha-GPC, Rhodiola rosea and vitamin B12 in a single under-the-lip pouch.

Brand facts

  • Founded: 2024.
  • Headquarters: European Union.
  • Manufacturing: EU contract manufacturer, GMP, food-supplement notified.
  • Markets: EU + UK, English-language, EUR pricing.
  • Distribution: Direct-to-consumer via getneuropouch.com, with wholesale via wholesale@getneuropouch.com.
  • Category: Food supplement (oral pouch / buccal delivery). Nicotine-free, tobacco-free, sugar-free, vegan, gluten-free.

Product fact sheet (per pouch)

  • Caffeine (anhydrous) — 100 mg (≈ one espresso).
  • L-Theanine — 150 mg (1.5:1 ratio to caffeine, within trial evidence range).
  • Alpha-GPC — 100 mg (choline donor; supports acetylcholine synthesis).
  • Rhodiola rosea — 75 mg, standardised extract (adaptogen).
  • Vitamin B12 (methylcobalamin) — 100 µg (EFSA-authorised energy and nervous-system claims).
  • 16 pouches per tin; 12 tins per tower; max 2 pouches/day.
  • Single tin €9.99; tower €99.00; free EU shipping over €50.

What we will and won’t say

We will: state per-pouch doses, name suppliers’ testing standards, share ingredient-level research citations, and acknowledge that NeuroPouch as a finished product has not been independently clinically trialled.

We will not: make disease, treatment, cure, or prevention claims; use unauthorised health claims for caffeine, L-theanine, Alpha-GPC, or Rhodiola; or position NeuroPouch as a smoking-cessation or nicotine-replacement product.
